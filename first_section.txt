# – highlighting headers at the beginning of a line
= or – – highlighting the headers of the first ("=") or second ("-") level (at least 3 characters in a row)
**Bold font** or _ Bold font_ - bold text selection
*Cursive font* or _cursive font_ - italicizing text
***Bold italics*** - text selection in bold italics
~~Crossed out text~~ - crossed out text
*String – unnumbered lists, a character at the beginning of the string
1, 2, 3 line – numbered lists
